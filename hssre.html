About Us

The custodians of Hindi Literature at IIT Kanpur.

Hindi Sahitya Sabha (HSS) is one of the oldest and most prestigious cultural clubs of IIT Kanpur. Established with the vision of preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Hindi literature, the club serves as a creative sanctuary for students.

We organize poetry recitations (Kavi Sammelan), literary debates, writing workshops, and guest lectures by renowned authors. Our mission is to prove that language is not just a medium of communication, but a vehicle of culture and emotion.

From the rustic charm of Premchand's stories to the fire of Dinkar's poetry, HSS celebrates every facet of Hindi literature. Join us in this journey of words, emotions, and expressions.
